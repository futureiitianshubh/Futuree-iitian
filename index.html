Your IIT Roadmap üöÄ

üìö Class 9: Foundation Phase
  • Master NCERT concepts thoroughly
  • Build strong fundamentals in Science & Math
  • Start solving practice problems consistently
  • Participate in Science Olympiads
  • Develop problem-solving skills gradually
  • Maintain 90%+ in board exams
  • Read books beyond syllabus for curiosity
  • Build discipline in study habits
üéØ Class 10: Balance & Excellence
  • Complete board syllabus with depth
  • Aim for 95%+ in board exams
  • Start light JEE preparation basics
  • Physics: Focus on laws and applications
  • Chemistry: Memorize + understand concepts
  • Math: Master problem-solving techniques
  • Attempt mock tests and sample papers
  • Join coaching if needed (guidance)
‚ö° Class 11: Serious Mode
  • Start formal JEE Main & Advanced prep
  • Study 4-5 hours daily after school
  • Physics: Mechanics, Thermodynamics focus
  • Chemistry: Organic, Inorganic, Physical
  • Math: Calculus, Algebra, Trigonometry
  • Solve previous year papers (10+ years)
  • Take full mock tests monthly
  • Maintain consistency over motivation
üí™ Class 12: Peak Performance
  • Balance board exams + JEE prep equally
  • Study 5-6 hours for JEE daily
  • Revise all concepts thoroughly
  • Take mock tests weekly
  • Analyze weak areas and improve
  • Score 90%+ in board exams
  • Manage stress and mental health
  • Appear in JEE Main (January & April)
üìñ Subject-Wise Strategy
  • Physics: Understand principles, apply to problems
  • Chemistry: Remember reactions, memorize series
  • Math: Master formulas, practice 100+ questions
  • Use multiple books (HC Verma, MS Chauhan, RD Sharma)
  • Watch YouTube explanations for doubts
  • Create formula sheets and notes
  • Revise weekly, test monthly
üß† Mindset & Discipline
  • Wake up at 5-6 AM daily (no excuses)
  • Study in chunks of 50-60 minutes
  • Take 10-15 minute breaks between sessions
  • Minimize phone/social media distractions
  • Sleep 7-8 hours for brain recovery
  • Eat healthy and exercise 30 mins daily
  • Believe in your preparation journey
  • Success = Hard Work + Smart Work + Time

Consistency Over Motivation
Motivation is temporary. Consistency is eternal. Show up every single day, even when you don't feel like it. Your future self will thank you for the discipline you build today. Small daily efforts compound into extraordinary results over time.

Mental Health & Burnout
It's okay to feel stressed. Take 10-minute breaks every hour. Exercise releases stress hormones. Talk to friends and family. Sleep is not laziness‚Äîit's recovery. If burnout hits, step back for a day, but never quit. Your mental health matters more than any exam.

Long-Term Thinking
Don't chase short-term wins. Build skills for life. Understand concepts deeply, not superficially. A 2-hour quality study beats 8 hours of distracted study. Think in terms of weeks and months, not days. The IIT exam is just one milestone in your long journey.
